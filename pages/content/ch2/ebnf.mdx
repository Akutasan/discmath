# EBNF

This tries to define formulas and statements with EBNF. Note that all literals are in double quotes.

_Symbol_ $\Leftarrow$ A-Z

_LogicalOperator_
    $\Leftarrow$ "$\land$"
        | "$\lor$"
        | "$\to$"
        | "$\lnot$"

_FormulaToStatementOperator_
    $\Leftarrow$ "$\equiv$"
        | "$\models$"

_StatementOperator_
    $\Leftarrow$ "$\implies$"
        | "and"
        | "or"
_Formula_
    $\Leftarrow$ _Symbol_
        | _Symbol_ _LogicalOperator_ _Symbol_
        | "("_Formula_ _LogicalOperator_ _Formula_")"
        | "$\top$"
        | "$\bot$"

_Statement_
    $\Leftarrow$ _Statement_ _StatementOperator_ _Statement_
        | _Statement_ "is false"
        | _Formula_ _FormulaToStatementOperator_ _Formula_
        | "$\models$" _Formula_
