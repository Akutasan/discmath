# Predicates in terms of membership


## Tuples

We introduce a special kind of set, the *tuple* or *pair*, which
stores a finite elements in a specific order. A tuple is defined as:
$$
(a, b) = \{ \{ a \}, \{ a, b \} \}
$$

<Callout type="def">
 **Cartesian product**:
  $$
    A \times B = \{ (a, b) \mid a \in A \land b \in B \}
  $$
</Callout>
